Urban Cadastre Survey of the Canton Seat and Parish Heads in Esmeraldas Canton, Esmeraldas Province

I directed comprehensive data collection, analysis, and mapping efforts, ensuring accurate information about the study area. Utilizing advanced GIS techniques, I created detailed cadastre records, showcasing my expertise in geographical analysis and urban mapping. This project highlights my proficiency in urban planning and GIS applications, further enriching my professional portfolio.

I contributed to a comprehensive territorial study and planning initiative centered around the El Shuyo area. Engaged in the creation of specialized thematic maps tailored to the unique requirements of the project.
Demonstrated expertise in geographical analysis and mapping techniques, enhancing the project's visual representation and analytical insights.



A base cartography was generated, as this information visually communicates spatial details, including dynamic three-dimensional spaces. This was achieved through the use of visual variables such as position, orientation, color, shape, texture, size, and intensity.
The collected information was gathered on the orthophoto.



To update the urban cadastre, information was collected on-site, including data from land and construction areas.



Once we obtained alphanumeric and graphical data, we established a database (SQL) to conduct quality control through validations, ensuring the accuracy and quality of the data.
