Maria Ortiz


Study of incidents of violent acts in the city of Esmeraldas that affect the data collection performance for updating the urban cadastre of Esmeraldas canton.

Thanks to the obtained results, strategies for data collection could be formulated more effectively and accurately. These strategies were based on the conclusions derived from the detailed analysis of the collected data, allowing the design of specific approaches to enhance the quality and relevance of the gathered information during the collection process. These strategic measures significantly contributed to optimizing the data collection process and ensuring the reliability of the gathered information for future analyses and decision-making.
